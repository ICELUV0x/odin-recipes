Caramel Apple Cheesecake recipe

Home

Description

Caramel apple meets cheesecake in this deliciously easy fall dessert that layers apple pie filling with cream cheese and caramel sauce to make a glorious filling for a graham cracker crust!

Ingredients

  • Apple pie filling: 1 can.
  • Graham cracker crust: 1 pcs (22 cm).
  • Cream cheese: 2 pack (220g), softened.
  • Sugar: 90g.
  • Vanila Extract: 1/4 tbs.
  • Eggs: 2 large.
  • Caramel sauce: 1/4 cup.
  • Pecan halves: 12 pcs.

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Preheat the oven to 175 degrees C (350 degrees F).
  3. Reserve 3/4 cup of apple pie filling and set aside. Spoon remaining pie filling into crust.
  4. Combine cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla in a large bowl; beat until smooth. Add eggs and mix well; Pour over apple filling in crust.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are puffed and surface of cheesecake is firm except for a small spot in the center that will jiggle when the pan is gently shaken, about 35 minutes. Remove cheesecake from oven and let it cool to room temperature.
  6. To make the caramel apple topping: Mix reserved apple filling and caramel sauce in a small saucepan over medium-low heat until warmed through, about 1 minute.
  7. Arrange apple slices around outside edge of cooled cheesecake. Spread caramel sauce evenly on top; Decorate with pecan halves around edge. Sprinkle with chopped pecans. Chill until ready to serve.
  8. Serve and enjoy!
